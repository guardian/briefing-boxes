What are the Cambridge Analytica Files

Working with a whistleblower who helped set up the company, the Guardian and its sister paper the Observer have obtained documents, contracts and eyewitness reports that lift the lid on Cambridge Analytica, the data firm that helped Donald Trump to victory and is the focus of investigations on both sides of the Atlantic by special prosecutor Robert Mueller in his inquiry into Trump-Russia collusion in the US and in the UK over its role in Brexit.
